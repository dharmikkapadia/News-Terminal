# ◢ MarketWire

**India's central-bank and markets wire, on one screen.**

MarketWire is a free web app for anyone who tracks the Reserve Bank of India and
Indian markets — analysts, traders, journalists, students. It gathers the official
announcements that move Indian markets into one live, readable news stream, and puts
the day's key numbers right above it.

**▶ [Open the live app](https://appapppy-dxslpadh7tudyueik8hedh.streamlit.app/)**

## What you get

- **One wire, three official sources.** RBI Press Releases, RBI Notifications, and
  SEBI Public Issues (new draft IPO filings) appear together in a single stream, each
  clearly tagged. Search by keyword, filter by source or date range, and sort
  newest-first or oldest-first.

- **News that doesn't disappear.** RBI's own feeds show only the latest handful of
  items and drop them as new ones publish. MarketWire keeps everything it has ever
  seen — and reaches back into RBI's archives for older releases — so you can scroll
  back through history, not just today's headlines.

- **The day's numbers at a glance.** An optional *Current Rates* dashboard shows the
  repo rate and policy corridor, CRR/SLR, the rupee against the dollar, euro and
  pound (with daily moves), the government-bond yield curve including the 10-year
  benchmark, Sensex and Nifty, and a live countdown to the next RBI monetary-policy
  (MPC) meeting.

- **Commodities at a glance.** An optional strip tracks Brent crude, gold, silver,
  copper, aluminium, zinc, steel, iron ore and coffee — each with its price, the
  day's percentage move (green/red), and a one-click link to a full chart.

- **The macro calendar, built in.** An optional economic calendar shows India's
  upcoming data releases — CPI, GDP, PMI, trade, RBI decisions and more — with
  consensus forecasts and importance ratings, plus the past week's results and
  whether each came in above or below expectations.

- **Reads like a news site.** A newspaper-style masthead over your choice of a
  single-column stream or a card grid, in a clean light markets-data design
  inspired by Trading Economics. The wire refreshes itself every few minutes;
  no reloading.

- **Shareable views.** Your source filters and layout are captured in the page URL —
  copy the link and a colleague opens exactly the view you're looking at.

## Where the data comes from

Announcements come straight from RBI and SEBI. Market numbers are drawn from public
sources — RBI/FBIL, Trading Economics, investing.com and Yahoo Finance — and refresh
automatically throughout the day.

MarketWire is an information tool, not investment advice. Always confirm figures
with the primary source before acting on them.
